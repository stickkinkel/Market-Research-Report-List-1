<p><p>Independent Software Vendors (ISVs) develop software solutions to meet specific customer needs. They operate in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on external servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and accessibility from various devices. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing higher control over the environment and data security. Each market type caters to different customer requirements and preferences in terms of maintenance, deployment, and operational costs.</p></p>
