<p><strong>The UV Filler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1K UV Filler</li><li>2K UV Filler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV filler market is primarily divided into two types: 1K UV filler and 2K UV filler. 1K UV fillers are ready-to-use products that cure when exposed to UV light, offering convenience and easy application. In contrast, 2K UV fillers require a hardener or catalyst to initiate the curing process, providing enhanced durability and adhesion. Both types are used in various industries, including automotive, electronics, and woodworking, catering to specific needs for surface finishing and protection.</p></p>
