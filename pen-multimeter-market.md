<p><strong>The Pen Multimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Multimeter</li><li>Pointer Multimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pen multimeter market encompasses two primary types: digital multimeters and pointer multimeters. Digital multimeters, known for their accuracy and ease of use, display measurements on a digital screen, making them ideal for precise readings of voltage, current, and resistance. In contrast, pointer multimeters, also called analog multimeters, utilize a needle and dial for readings. While they may offer less precision, they provide real-time response and are often favored for educational purposes and basic troubleshooting tasks.</p></p>
