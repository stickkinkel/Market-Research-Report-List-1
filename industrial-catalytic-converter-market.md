<p><strong>The Industrial Catalytic Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Way</li><li>Three-Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial catalytic converter market is primarily categorized into two types: Two-Way and Three-Way converters. Two-Way converters are designed to facilitate oxidation reactions, primarily converting carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons into carbon dioxide and water. In contrast, Three-Way converters perform both oxidation and reduction processes, efficiently transforming nitrogen oxides alongside carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons into harmless nitrogen and oxygen, along with carbon dioxide. These technologies are crucial for reducing harmful emissions from industrial operations, promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
