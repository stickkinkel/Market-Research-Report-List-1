<p><strong>The Evidential Alcohol Testing Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Evidential Alcohol Testing Devices (EATDs) are categorized into two main market types: contact and contactless. Contact devices require physical interaction, typically involving a mouthpiece that the user breathes into, ensuring precise BAC readings. They are commonly used by law enforcement at checkpoints. In contrast, contactless devices utilize advanced technology, such as infrared or radar, allowing for the assessment of alcohol levels without direct contact. This method enhances convenience and speed, making it suitable for various settings like events or workplaces.</p></p>
