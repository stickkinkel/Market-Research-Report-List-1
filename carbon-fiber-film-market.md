<p><p>The carbon fiber film market is categorized into three main types: single-walled, double-walled, and multi-walled. Single-walled carbon fiber films consist of a single layer of carbon fibers, offering superior flexibility and strength for lightweight applications. Double-walled films feature two layers, enhancing thermal and electrical properties, suitable for more demanding environments. Multi-walled films consist of multiple layered structures, providing increased durability and performance, making them ideal for high-stress applications in sectors like aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment.</p></p>
