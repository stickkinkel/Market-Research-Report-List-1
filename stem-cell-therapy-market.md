<p><ul><li>Autologous</li><li>Allogeneic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stem cell therapy market is categorized into two main types: autologous and allogeneic. Autologous stem cell therapy involves using a patient’s own stem cells for treatment, minimizing the risk of rejection and complications. This type is often used in regenerative medicine and certain cancer treatments. In contrast, allogeneic stem cell therapy utilizes stem cells from a donor, enabling treatment for patients who may not have enough viable cells. This approach is commonly used in conditions like leukemia and other blood disorders.</p></p>
