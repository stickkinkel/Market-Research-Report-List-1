<p><p>The Unmanned Underwater Vehicle (UUV) market consists of two primary types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are tethered systems operated by a pilot from the surface, commonly used for tasks like underwater inspections and repairs. In contrast, AUVs are self-guided and operate independently, equipped for tasks such as environmental monitoring and mapping. Both types serve critical roles in marine exploration, defense, and research, driving advancements in technology and expanding operational capabilities in underwater environments.</p></p>
