<p><p>Bentonite mining involves the extraction of various types of bentonite clay, primarily categorized into sodium and calcium bentonite. Sodium bentonite is known for its high swelling properties, making it ideal for applications such as drilling fluids and sealants. Calcium bentonite, on the other hand, is less swellable and often used in agriculture and industrial applications. Additionally, other types may include potassium or aluminum bentonite, each serving specialized purposes in diverse sectors like cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and construction.</p></p>
