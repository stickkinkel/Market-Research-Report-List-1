<p><strong>The Phosphor Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Decay</li><li>Long Decay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphor screen market is divided into two main types based on decay characteristics: short decay and long decay phosphors. Short decay phosphors emit light rapidly, providing quick visual responses ideal for applications requiring immediate feedback, such as in display technologies and medical imaging. In contrast, long decay phosphors release light over an extended period, making them suitable for persistent illumination applications, like safety signage and certain types of glow-in-the-dark products. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
