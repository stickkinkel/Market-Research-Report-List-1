<p><p>Handheld Laser Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS) is an advanced analytical technique used for determining the elemental composition of materials. This portable technology utilizes laser pulses to ablate a small amount of the sample, creating a plasma that emits light. The emitted light is then analyzed to identify the elements present in the sample, making LIBS highly effective for applications in fields such as environmental monitoring, mining, and material evaluation.</p><p>The Handheld Laser Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for portable analytical tools and advancements in laser technology. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. The expansion of applications in sectors like aerospace, pharmaceuticals, and environmental science further propels market growth.
