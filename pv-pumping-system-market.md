<p><ul><li>Submersible</li><li>Surface Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV Pumping System market consists of two main types: submersible pumps and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to be submerged beneath the water source, effectively lifting water for agricultural or domestic use, making them ideal for deep wells. Surface pumps, on the other hand, are installed above ground and draw water from a surface source like rivers or tanks, suitable for shallower applications. Both systems leverage solar energy to enhance efficiency and reduce operational costs in water management.</p></p>
