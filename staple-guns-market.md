<p><p>The staple gun market is categorized into three main types: manual, electrical, and pneumatic staple guns. Manual staple guns are handheld tools powered by user strength, ideal for light tasks. Electrical staple guns offer increased efficiency and speed, powered by electricity, suitable for medium to heavy-duty applications. Pneumatic staple guns utilize compressed air for high-speed stapling and are often used in industrial settings, delivering power and precision for heavy-duty projects. Each type caters to specific user needs and applications.</p></p>
