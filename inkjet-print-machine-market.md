<p><strong>The Inkjet Print Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet Technology</li><li>Random Inkjet Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet print machine market is primarily divided into two types: Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Random Inkjet (also known as Drop-on-Demand or DOD). Continuous Inkjet technology maintains a continuous flow of ink, expelling droplets that are directed onto the substrate, making it ideal for high-speed industrial applications. In contrast, Random Inkjet technology sprays ink only when needed, allowing for precise control over droplet placement, making it suitable for detailed images and variable text applications in packaging and commercial printing.</p></p>
