<p><ul><li>Vertical Spindle</li><li>Rotating Disk</li><li>Movable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Core drilling machines are categorized into three main types based on their design and functionality. Vertical spindle drills utilize a vertically positioned spindle to achieve precision in drilling operations, ideal for dense materials. Rotating disk core drills employ a circular cutting edge that allows for efficient material removal, making them suitable for larger diameter holes. Movable drills offer flexibility and portability, enabling operators to adjust their position and drilling angle easily, enhancing usability across various applications and job sites.</p></p>
