<p><strong>The Corian Acrylic Solid Surface Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting Molding Solid Surface</li><li>Extrusion Molding Solid Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Corian Acrylic Solid Surface market encompasses two primary types: casting molding and extrusion molding solid surfaces. Casting molding involves pouring a liquid acrylic mixture into molds to create complex shapes, resulting in high-quality, seamless surfaces ideal for countertops and fixtures. In contrast, extrusion molding produces continuous sheets by forcing the acrylic through a die, allowing for efficient production of standardized sizes and shapes. Both methods offer durable, versatile, and aesthetically appealing surfaces, widely used in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
