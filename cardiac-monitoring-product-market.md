<p><p>The cardiac monitoring product market includes several key types. Mobile cardiac telemetry provides real-time data transmission of heart activity via mobile devices. Event monitoring records specific heart events triggered by patient activities, while Holter monitoring continuously tracks heart rhythms over 24 to 48 hours. Extended Holter monitoring extends this duration, allowing for longer-term analysis, often up to several weeks. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses devices and technologies that do not fit neatly into these classifications, providing diverse solutions for cardiac care.</p></p>
