<p><p>The medical magnet market features three primary types: NdFeB, Sintered NdFeB, and Ferrite. NdFeB (Neodymium Iron Boron) magnets are known for their exceptional strength and miniaturization capabilities, making them ideal for advanced medical devices. Sintered NdFeB magnets are manufactured through a sintering process, enhancing their magnetic properties while maintaining high performance. Ferrite magnets, typically less expensive, offer good corrosion resistance and moderate strength, making them suitable for various lower-cost medical applications. Each type caters to specific healthcare needs and technological demands.</p></p>
