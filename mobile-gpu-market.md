<p><strong>The Mobile GPU Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Independent GPU</li><li>Integrated GPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile GPU market comprises two main types: independent GPUs and integrated GPUs. Independent GPUs are dedicated graphics processors that function separately from the CPU, offering superior performance for gaming and graphics-intensive applications. They enhance multitasking and deliver high-quality visuals. In contrast, integrated GPUs are built into the CPU, sharing system memory, which makes them more power-efficient and cost-effective but less powerful. Integrated GPUs are suitable for everyday tasks and light gaming, appealing to budget-conscious consumers.</p></p>
