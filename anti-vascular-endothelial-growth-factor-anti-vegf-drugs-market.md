<p><p>The Anti-VEGF drugs market includes several types, each targeting angiogenesis. Agonists stimulate VEGF receptors, promoting growth, while allosteric modulators alter receptor activity to regulate responses. Kinase inhibitors disrupt signaling pathways involved in vascular growth. Monoclonal antibodies specifically bind to VEGF, blocking its interaction with receptors to inhibit tumor blood supply. Other emerging therapies may include small molecules or combination therapies that enhance the effectiveness of existing anti-VEGF treatments, addressing various cancer and retinal diseases.</p></p>
