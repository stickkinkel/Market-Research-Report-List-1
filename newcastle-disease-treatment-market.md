<p><p>The Newcastle Disease Treatment Market is categorized into three main types of vaccines: live attenuated, inactivated, and conjugated vaccines. Live attenuated vaccines contain weakened forms of the virus, stimulating strong immune responses without causing disease. Inactivated vaccines use killed virus particles, providing safety but typically requiring multiple doses for effectiveness. Conjugated vaccines combine viral components with carrier proteins to enhance immune response, particularly in young or immunocompromised birds. These vaccine types are crucial for preventing Newcastle Disease in poultry populations.</p></p>
