They are often used on angle grinders for tasks like refining concrete and stone surfaces. Grinding discs, on the other hand, are thinner and more versatile, suitable for cutting, shaping, and smoothing various materials. Both types are essential tools in construction, metalworking, and woodworking industries.</p></p>
