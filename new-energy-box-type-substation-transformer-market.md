<p><p>The New Energy Box-type Substation Transformer market is primarily categorized into two types: Oil Immersed Transformers and Dry-type Transformers. Oil Immersed Transformers utilize oil for cooling and insulation, making them suitable for high capacity and diverse environmental conditions, though they require more maintenance. In contrast, Dry-type Transformers use air for cooling, enhancing safety and reducing fire risk, but typically have lower capacity. Both types are essential for efficient energy management in modern electrical grids, supporting renewable energy integration.</p></p>
