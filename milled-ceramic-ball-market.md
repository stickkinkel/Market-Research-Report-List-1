<p><p>Milled ceramic balls are essential in various industrial applications, classified mainly into inert and active ceramic balls. Inert ceramic balls, typically used as packing materials in reactors, provide structural support without engaging in chemical reactions. They enhance efficiency and reduce the risk of contamination. Active ceramic balls, on the other hand, possess reactive properties that facilitate specific chemical processes, improving reaction rates and product yields. Both types cater to diverse needs in industries such as petrochemicals, pharmaceuticals, and environmental engineering.</p></p>
