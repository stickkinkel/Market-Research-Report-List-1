<p><ul><li>Sustain Pedal</li><li>Multifunction Pedal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic piano pedal market primarily consists of two main types: sustain pedals and multifunction pedals. Sustain pedals are designed to replicate the damper effect found in acoustic pianos, allowing notes to resonate longer. They are crucial for expressive playing. Multifunction pedals, on the other hand, offer various capabilities beyond sustain, including volume control, tonal variation, and program changes, enhancing a pianist's versatility. Both types contribute significantly to the overall experience of playing electronic pianos.</p></p>
