<p><p>The Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) equipment market encompasses various components essential for effective drilling operations. Reamers are used to enlarge the borehole for the installation of pipes, while openers facilitate the transition between different sections. Other equipment includes guidance systems and fluid circulation tools, enhancing overall drilling efficiency.</p></p>
