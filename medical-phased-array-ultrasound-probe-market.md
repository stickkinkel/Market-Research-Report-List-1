<p><strong>The Medical Phased Array Ultrasound Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Type</li><li>Face Type</li><li>Ring Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical phased array ultrasound probe market is categorized into three main types: line type, face type, and ring type. Line type probes consist of a linear arrangement of transducers, allowing for high-resolution images over a narrow area. Face type probes have a flat transducer array that enables wider field imaging, suitable for larger anatomical structures. Ring type probes feature a circular arrangement of sensors, providing 360-degree imaging capability, ideal for challenging anatomical evaluations and advanced imaging techniques.</p></p>
