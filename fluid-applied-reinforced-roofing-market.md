<p><p>The Fluid Applied Reinforced Roofing market includes three primary types: acrylics, silicones, and urethanes. Acrylics are water-based, providing a flexible and UV-resistant solution, making them ideal for various climates. Silicones offer excellent waterproofing and high resistance to extreme weather, ideal for flat roofs. Urethanes provide robust durability and superior adhesion, making them suitable for high-traffic areas and extreme conditions. Each type serves distinct performance needs, contributing to overall roof longevity and energy efficiency.</p></p>
