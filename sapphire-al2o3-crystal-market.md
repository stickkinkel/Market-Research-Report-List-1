<p><p>The sapphire crystal market comprises primarily two types: Skarn type sapphire and hydrothermal type sapphire. Skarn type sapphires form through high-temperature metamorphic processes involving limestone and are typically found in metamorphosed areas, featuring unique colors and patterns. Hydrothermal type sapphires, on the other hand, are synthesized in controlled environments that mimic natural conditions, allowing for consistent quality and purity. Both types cater to various applications, from jewelry to industrial uses, reflecting their unique origins and properties.</p></p>
