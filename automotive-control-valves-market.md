<p><p>The automotive control valves market is primarily categorized into electric and hydraulic types. Electric control valves utilize electrical signals to regulate fluid flow, offering precise control and faster response times, often favored in modern vehicles for efficiency and automation. Hydraulic control valves, on the other hand, rely on hydraulic pressure to operate, providing robustness and reliability in heavy-duty applications. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing engine performance, enhancing safety features, and improving overall vehicle functionality.</p></p>
