<p><p>The lymphoma molecular diagnostics market encompasses various testing methods to identify and analyze lymphoma types at a genetic level. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences, enabling the detection of genetic mutations. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) visualizes chromosomal abnormalities using fluorescent probes. DNA sequencing provides comprehensive insights into the genetic makeup of lymphoma cells. Gene chips analyze expression levels of multiple genes simultaneously, offering a broad understanding of the molecular characteristics of lymphoma, aiding in diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
