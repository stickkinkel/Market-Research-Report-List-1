<p><strong>The Dental Night Guard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Preformed</li><li>Thermoformed</li><li>Custom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental night guard market comprises three main types: preformed, thermoformed, and custom night guards. Preformed night guards are ready-made and available for immediate use, offering convenience but limited fit. Thermoformed night guards are soft materials shaped by heating, providing a better fit but requiring some effort to mold. Custom night guards are professionally made based on individual dental impressions, offering optimal comfort and protection. Each type varies in terms of fit, comfort, and usability, catering to different user needs.</p></p>
