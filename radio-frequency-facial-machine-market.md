<p><p>The Radio Frequency Facial Machine market is segmented into three main types: Monopolar, Bipolar, and Multiple Polar Radio Frequency. Monopolar RF uses a single electrode for deep tissue heating, effective for tightening and lifting. Bipolar RF employs two electrodes, targeting superficial layers for improved skin texture and tone. Multiple Polar RF combines various electrode configurations to deliver versatile treatment options, enhancing both depth and precision in skin rejuvenation. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to different skincare needs and preferences.</p></p>
