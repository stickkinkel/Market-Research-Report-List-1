<p><strong>The Screw Piles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SS-RS combinations Type Screw Piles</li><li>SS (Square Shaft) Type Screw Piles</li><li>RS (Round Shaft) Type Screw Piles</li><li>Helical Grouted Displacement Piles (HGD Piles)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screw piles are foundational elements designed for efficiency in soil conditions. The SS-RS combinations combine square and round shaft designs, optimizing load-bearing capacities. SS-type screw piles feature a square shaft providing superior resistance, while RS-type offers ease of installation with round shafts. Helical Grouted Displacement (HGD) piles enhance stability by using grout to fill the dislocated soil around the helix, ensuring effective load transfer. Each type serves unique applications, influenced by soil properties and structural requirements.</p></p>
