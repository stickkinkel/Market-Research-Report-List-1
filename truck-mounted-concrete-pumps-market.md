<p><ul><li>Line Pumps</li><li>Boom Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck-mounted concrete pumps market primarily includes line pumps and boom pumps, each serving distinct applications in construction. Line pumps utilize hoses to transport concrete horizontally, ideal for small to medium-scale projects or hard-to-reach areas. Conversely, boom pumps feature a robotic arm that extends vertically and horizontally, allowing for precise placement of concrete over obstacles, making them suitable for large-scale constructions like high-rise buildings. Both pump types enhance efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve the quality of concrete placement.</p></p>
