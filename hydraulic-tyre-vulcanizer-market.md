<p><strong>The Hydraulic Tyre Vulcanizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AFV</li><li>BOM</li><li>AVBO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hydraulic Tyre Vulcanizer market includes three main types: AFV (Auto Fire Vulcanizer), BOM (Batch Operating Machine), and AVBO (Automatic Vulcanizing Boiler). AFV is designed for high-volume production with automated features, enhancing efficiency. BOM focuses on smaller batches, suitable for custom or specialized tyre production, allowing flexibility. AVBO combines automated processes with a boiler system to improve heat distribution and cure time, ensuring consistent quality. Each type serves distinct production needs within the tyre manufacturing sector.</p></p>
