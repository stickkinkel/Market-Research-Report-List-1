Medium voltage circuit breakers, operating between 1kV to 100kV, are used in industrial and commercial applications for equipment safety. Low voltage circuit breakers, functioning below 1kV, protect residential and small commercial circuits, ensuring safety from overloads and short circuits in everyday electrical systems.</p></p>
