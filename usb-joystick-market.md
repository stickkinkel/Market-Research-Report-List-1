<p><p>The USB joystick market is categorized into single-axis and multi-axis joysticks. Single-axis joysticks are designed for control along one direction, typically used in simple applications like arcade games or basic simulation controls. In contrast, multi-axis joysticks offer control across multiple dimensions, allowing for more complex movements and finer control. This makes them ideal for advanced gaming, flight simulators, and robotics. Both types cater to varied user needs, influencing their respective market demand and applications.</p></p>
