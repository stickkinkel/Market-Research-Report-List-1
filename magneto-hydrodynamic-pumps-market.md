<p><p>The Magneto Hydrodynamic (MHD) Pumps market is categorized into two main types: Permanent Magnet Type Hydrodynamic Pumps and Magnetic Coil Type Hydrodynamic Pumps. Permanent Magnet Type Pumps utilize permanent magnets to create a magnetic field, facilitating fluid movement without mechanical components. In contrast, Magnetic Coil Type Pumps employ electromagnetic coils to generate the necessary magnetic field for propulsion. Both types are prominent for their efficiency, reliability, and ability to handle corrosive fluids, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
