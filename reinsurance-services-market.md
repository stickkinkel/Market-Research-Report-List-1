<p><p>Reinsurance services encompass two main market types: property and casualty (P&C) reinsurance and life reinsurance. P&C reinsurance provides coverage for insurers against losses from property damage and liability claims, helping stabilize their financial performance in volatile environments. Life reinsurance, on the other hand, pertains to policies covering mortality risks and longevity, allowing insurers to manage risk exposure and enhance capital efficiency. Both sectors play crucial roles in the insurance ecosystem, ensuring stability and solvency for insurers.</p></p>
