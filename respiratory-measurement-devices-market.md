<p><p>The respiratory measurement devices market encompasses various tools used to evaluate and monitor respiratory function. Pulse oximeters measure blood oxygen saturation levels non-invasively. Spirometers assess lung function by measuring air volume and flow rates. Capnographs monitor carbon dioxide levels in exhaled air, providing insights into respiratory efficiency. Peak flow meters gauge the maximum speed of expelled air, aiding in the management of asthma. Collectively, these devices are vital for diagnosing and managing respiratory conditions effectively.</p></p>
