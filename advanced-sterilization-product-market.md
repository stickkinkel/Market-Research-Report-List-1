<p><p>The Advanced Sterilization Products market includes various sterilization types: </p><p>1. **Dry Heat** sterilization utilizes high temperatures to eliminate microorganisms, often used for metal instruments.</p><p>2. **Ethylene Oxide** is a gas-based method effective for heat-sensitive items, proving versatile for complex shapes.</p><p>3. **E-Beam** employs electron beams for rapid sterilization, suitable for single-use medical devices.</p><p>4. **Steam** sterilization, or autoclaving, uses pressurized steam to ensure thorough decontamination, ideal for various surgical tools. Each method serves specific needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
