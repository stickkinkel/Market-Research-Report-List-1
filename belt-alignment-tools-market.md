<p><strong>The Belt Alignment Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser–emitting Unit</li><li>Receiver Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt alignment tools are essential for ensuring the proper alignment of conveyor belts and rotating equipment. The market is primarily divided into two types: laser-emitting units and receiver units. Laser-emitting units project a laser beam along the belt’s length, helping to identify misalignments visually. Receiver units detect the emitted laser beam to confirm alignment accuracy. Together, they provide a reliable and precise method for maintaining optimal belt performance, reducing wear and tear, and enhancing overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
