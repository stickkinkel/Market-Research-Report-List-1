<p><strong>The Rotary Screen Changer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Piston</li><li>Double Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary screen changer market is primarily divided into two types: single piston and double piston systems. Single piston screen changers utilize one piston to manage the flow of material and change screens, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, double piston screen changers employ two pistons, allowing for continuous operation and higher productivity by enabling simultaneous screen changes without interrupting the material flow. Both types serve industries such as plastics and rubber, enhancing filtration efficiency and process reliability.</p></p>
