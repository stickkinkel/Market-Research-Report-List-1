<p><p>CNC transfer machines are classified into vertical and horizontal axis types based on their operational orientation. Vertical axis machines feature a vertically oriented spindle, ideal for handling heavy workpieces and offering precision in milling and drilling applications. In contrast, horizontal axis machines utilize a horizontally aligned spindle, enhancing accessibility and facilitating multi-stage machining processes. Both types optimize production efficiency by automating the transfer of workpieces through various machining stations, catering to diverse manufacturing needs in industries like automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
