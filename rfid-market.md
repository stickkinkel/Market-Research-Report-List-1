<p><strong>The RFID Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID market is primarily divided into two types: passive RFID and active RFID. Passive RFID systems use tags that do not have their power source; they rely on energy from the reader's signal, making them cost-effective and ideal for applications like inventory management and asset tracking. Active RFID, in contrast, features battery-powered tags that can broadcast signals over longer distances, making them suitable for real-time tracking in logistics or larger environments. Each type serves distinct use cases based on range and cost-efficiency.</p></p>
