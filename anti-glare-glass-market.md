Single-sided High Transmittance Level features one treated side for enhanced light transmission and glare reduction, ideal for high-demand environments like offices. Double-sided High Transmittance Level provides treatment on both sides, maximizing clarity and minimizing glare for applications requiring optimal visibility, such as high-end displays and photography.</p></p>
